Regulator coverage matrix

Every major UAE regulator, one partner

We map your business model to the correct authority before any application begins — across virtual assets, securities, financial services, gaming and banking.

VARA

Virtual Asset Regulatory Authority

Dubai's dedicated virtual-assets regulator for VASPs, exchanges, custody and broker-dealers.

SCA

Securities & Commodities Authority

Federal capital-markets regulator covering securities, commodities and tokenized assets.

DFSA

Dubai Financial Services Authority

Independent regulator for financial services conducted in or from the DIFC.

ADGM

Abu Dhabi Global Market · FSRA

Abu Dhabi's international financial centre and its financial services regulator.

GCGRA

Commercial Gaming Regulatory Authority

Federal regulator for commercial gaming and lotteries across the UAE.

CBUAE

Central Bank of the UAE

Authority for banking, payment services and stored-value facilities.
